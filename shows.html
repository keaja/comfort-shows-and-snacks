My top Shows to binge watch while snacking

This is a list of a few of my comfort shows to watch

  • King of the Hill
  • As told by Ginger
  • Vampire Diaries
  • Spirited Away
  • Howl's Moving Castle

Growing up watching these shows holds fond memories of my childhood, each show is paired with a favorite meal or snack from my childhood. Watching King of the hill at night reminds me of the dinners I ate with my family before bed, as told by ginger the snacks i would eat watching it after school. Spirited away was one of my first introductions to anime movies, eating my snacks and comparing them to food I saw on the screen. Howl's moving castle was a fun watch paired with snacks for the ride of the movie.

  • lunchables
  • corn dogs
  • bagel bites
  • corner store snacks
